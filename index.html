Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design and consultation process?
+
We begin with a complimentary private consultation at your home. Our designer spends 60–90 minutes understanding your vision, lifestyle, and goals. We then prepare a bespoke design concept with material selections, 3D renderings, and a detailed investment proposal — typically within 7–10 business days.
Do you work with interior designers and architects?
+
Absolutely. We actively collaborate with San Antonio's leading interior designers and architects. We're accustomed to working within established design frameworks and coordinating with other professionals to achieve a unified vision for your home.
How long does a luxury kitchen remodel typically take?
+
A full luxury kitchen transformation typically requires 4–8 weeks for construction, following 2–4 weeks for design finalization and material procurement. We provide a detailed project timeline before any work begins, and your dedicated project director will keep you informed at every milestone.
Can you source specific luxury materials or brands I have in mind?
+
Yes. We have established relationships with premium material suppliers across the United States and internationally. Whether you've seen a specific Italian marble, a custom European cabinetry line, or a particular appliance brand, we can source it for your project.
What does your 5-year warranty cover?
+
Our 5-year artisan warranty covers all workmanship including installation, craftsmanship, and structural integrity of our work. Material warranties are passed through directly from manufacturers and are often 10–25 years. We handle all warranty claims as your single point of contact.
Do we need to vacate during the remodel?
+
For kitchen remodels, temporary kitchen arrangements are typically needed for 4–6 weeks. For bathroom remodels, we work in phases when possible to maintain access to at least one functioning bathroom. We discuss all logistics during your consultation to ensure minimal disruption to your household.
