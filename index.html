Questions Homeowners Ask

Kitchen and bathroom remodeling questions in San Antonio.

These are some of the first things people want to know before booking the design visit or sending over project photos.

What should I send before the first visit?

Project photos, rough measurements, your neighborhood, and the room you want to change are enough to start the conversation well.

Do you work in Stone Oak, Alamo Heights, The Dominion, Boerne, and nearby?

Yes. The site is built around the San Antonio areas where homeowners are looking for more thoughtful kitchen and bathroom remodeling work.

Do you work with Schluter, RedGard, James Hardie, Sherwin-Williams, Kohler, Delta, or Moen?

Yes, when those products fit the scope and finish direction of the project. Product selection is matched to the room, the materials, the color direction, and the level of finish the homeowner wants.

Can we start with one bathroom, one kitchen, or one closet?

Absolutely. Some projects begin with one room and later grow into multiple spaces once the first phase is complete.
