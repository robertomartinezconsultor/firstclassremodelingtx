Modernizing Your Bathroom on a Budget

A bathroom remodel can range from a few thousand dollars to over $20,000, depending on the scope of the project. If you're looking to modernize your bathroom without breaking the bank, consider updating your fixtures, such as your sink, toilet, and showerhead, which can cost between $500 to $2,000. You can also give your bathroom a fresh new look by painting the walls and updating the lighting, which can cost around $1,000 to $3,000. In neighborhoods like Stone Oak, where homes tend to have a more modern aesthetic, a budget-friendly remodel can still make a significant impact.

Luxury Bathroom Remodels for the Ultimate Retreat

If you're looking to create a luxurious bathroom oasis, the possibilities are endless. Consider installing a freestanding tub, a large walk-in shower, or a double vanity, which can cost between $5,000 to $15,000 or more, depending on the materials and design. In affluent neighborhoods like Alamo Heights and Dominion, homeowners often opt for high-end finishes and amenities, such as marble countertops, heated floors, and smart home technology.
